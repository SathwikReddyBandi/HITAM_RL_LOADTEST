How to use this dashboard

  • Overview
  • Turn on the power supply and load buttons.
  • Take down the No-load readings and make a note of No-load voltage
  • Turn on the load switch.
  • Take down the V, I & W of primary and secondary sides.
  • Increase the load in steps using the increment button present in the dashboard.
  • Note: The load can be increased only when the previous load values are printed in table.
  • Take down the eventual readings.
  • Plot the curves for %Î· Vs load power and %Vreg Vs load power.
  • To restart the experiment use reset key and can start the experiment from the beginning.
  • Note: There's a latency of about 5-10 seconds in video feed.
